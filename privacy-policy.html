Parental Screen Monitoring

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy and your family's safety seriously. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how.

Effective: March 2026

1 Who We Are

SooperVision is a parental screen monitoring application developed and operated by SooperVision ("we", "us", or "our"). The app allows a parent or guardian (the "Viewer") to monitor the screen of a child's device (the "Host") in real time, with the full knowledge and consent of the device owner.

By using SooperVision, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: First name, last name, date of birth provided during registration
  • Contact information: Phone number and/or email address used to create your account
  • Authentication data: Firebase Authentication tokens (phone OTP or email/password)
  • Subscription data: Plan type (monthly/yearly), subscription start and expiry dates, Razorpay payment IDs
  • Usage data: Trial start timestamp, login timestamps, app activity logs
  • Screen content: Live screen data is transmitted in real time from the Host device to the Viewer using WebRTC. We do not store, record, or retain any screen content on our servers.
Important: Screen sharing is a live, real-time peer-to-peer connection. No video or screenshots are ever saved by SooperVision.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify your identity via phone OTP or email
  • Provide the screen monitoring service between paired devices
  • Process subscription payments via Razorpay
  • Enforce trial periods and subscription validity
  • Send service-related notifications (e.g. subscription expiry reminders)
  • Improve the app and understand how users interact with it
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

4 Screen Monitoring — Consent & Transparency

SooperVision is designed exclusively for parental monitoring of minor children's devices with the knowledge of all parties involved. The app is not intended to be used covertly.

  • The Host device (child's device) must have the app installed and screen sharing must be actively started by the device user
  • A persistent notification is displayed on the Host device at all times while sharing is active
  • The Host device user can stop sharing at any time
  • Using this app to monitor any person without their knowledge or consent is strictly prohibited and may be illegal in your jurisdiction
You are solely responsible for ensuring you have appropriate legal authority to monitor any device using this app.

5 Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firebase Realtime Database and Firebase Authentication), which is hosted on Google's infrastructure with industry-standard encryption.

  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS and WSS (secure WebSocket)
  • Firebase Authentication manages all login credentials — we never store raw passwords
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Razorpay — we only store the Razorpay payment ID reference, never card numbers or UPI details
  • Screen data is transmitted peer-to-peer via WebRTC and is never stored on any server

6 Data Sharing with Third Parties

We share data only with the following trusted service providers, solely to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database storage (Google Privacy Policy applies)
  • Razorpay — payment processing (Razorpay Privacy Policy applies)

We do not share your data with advertisers, analytics companies, or any other third parties.

7 Children's Privacy

SooperVision is an app for parents and guardians. The app account holder must be 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 as account holders.

The Host device (which may be used by a child) only shares live screen data — no personal data about the child is collected or stored by our systems beyond the screen stream itself, which is never retained.

8 Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

Upon account deletion:

  • All profile information is permanently removed from Firebase
  • Authentication credentials are deleted
  • Subscription records are removed (this does not trigger a refund)

9 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Effective" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes, we will notify users via the app or email.
